# Product Overview

## What is this?

A modern, interactive portfolio website for Temitope Williams—a product designer and project manager showcasing their work, services, and expertise.

## Core Purpose

- Showcase professional work and projects
- Highlight services: product design, project management, mobile app development, and consultation
- Enable potential clients to get in touch
- Demonstrate design and technical excellence through the portfolio itself

## Key Features

- **Hero Section**: Eye-catching introduction with parallax effects
- **Work/Projects Section**: Gallery of completed projects with modal details
- **About Section**: Professional background and statistics
- **Services Section**: Four core service offerings with descriptions
- **Contact Section**: Call-to-action with contact modal
- **Interactive Elements**: Custom cursor, mouse parallax effects, smooth animations
- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-first approach with full responsiveness

## Design Philosophy

- **Minimalist & Modern**: Clean aesthetic with strategic use of color accents
- **Interactive**: Subtle parallax, hover effects, and smooth animations enhance engagement
- **Accessible**: Respects reduced motion preferences, semantic HTML
- **Performance-Focused**: Optimized images, efficient animations, smooth scrolling

## Target Audience

Potential clients and collaborators looking to hire a product designer or project manager.
